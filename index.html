Penetration Testing (Ethical Hacking)

We simulate real-world cyberattacks to identify security vulnerabilities before hackers exploit them. Our ethical hacking services include:

Web Application Penetration Testing – Testing websites and apps for security flaws.
Network Penetration Testing – Identifying weaknesses in wired and wireless networks.
Benefit: Detect and fix security weaknesses before real attackers find them.

Threat Assessment & Risk Management

We help organizations assess cybersecurity risks and implement strategies to mitigate potential threats. Our services include:
Cyber Risk Assessments – Identifying weak points in IT infrastructure.
Vulnerability Scanning – Regular scans for security gaps in software and networks.
Benefit: Stay ahead of cybercriminals by proactively managing risks.

Security Audits & Compliance

We ensure your business meets industry security standards and compliance regulations.
Our audit services cover:
IT Security Audits – Evaluating security controls and infrastructure.
Regulatory Compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001, NIST, PCI-DSS) – Ensuring your business follows required security guidelines.
Benefit: Avoid fines and penalties by meeting legal and security standards.
